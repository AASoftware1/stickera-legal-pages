Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 15, 2024

Your Privacy Matters: Stickera is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Account creation date

1.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about your use of the App:

  • Stickers you create and generate
  • AI prompts and generation requests
  • Credit usage and transaction history
  • App features you use
  • Device information (type, OS version)
  • App version and crash reports

1.3 Content You Create

  • AI-generated stickers (images and videos)
  • Text prompts and generation parameters
  • Edited versions of stickers
  • Metadata associated with your content

1.4 Payment Information

When you make in-app purchases:

  • Payment processing is handled by Apple (App Store)
  • We receive only transaction confirmation and receipt data
  • We do NOT store your credit card or payment details

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Service: Process AI generation requests, store your stickers, manage your account
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
  • Manage Subscriptions: Track credits, process payments, handle subscription renewals
  • Communicate: Send important updates, service announcements, and customer support responses
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to operate the App:

3.1 Supabase (Backend Infrastructure)

  • Stores user accounts, stickers, and metadata
  • Hosted in secure cloud servers
  • Privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

3.2 FAL.ai (AI Image Generation)

  • Processes text-to-image generation requests
  • Receives your prompts and generation parameters
  • Privacy policy: https://fal.ai/privacy

3.4 Apple In-App Purchase

  • Processes all payment transactions
  • Privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

3.5 Adapty (Subscription Management)

  • Manages subscription states and analytics
  • Privacy policy: https://adapty.io/privacy

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Your Data

  • Account data: Supabase cloud servers (encrypted)
  • Stickers: Supabase cloud storage (publicly accessible URLs)
  • Local cache: Your device (for performance)

4.2 Security Measures

  • Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard hashing
  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Database access is restricted with row-level security policies
  • Regular security audits and updates

4.3 Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Stickers: Retained while your account is active or until you delete them
  • Transaction history: Retained for accounting and legal purposes (minimum 7 years)
  • Deleted data: Permanently removed within 30 days of deletion

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Download

You can access and download all your stickers from the Gallery at any time.

5.2 Delete Content

You can delete individual stickers from the Gallery. Deleted stickers are permanently removed from our servers.

5.3 Delete Account

You can request account deletion by contacting us at support@stickera.app. This will permanently delete:

  • Your account information
  • All your stickers
  • Credit balance and transaction history

Note: Active subscriptions must be cancelled separately through Apple App Store.

5.4 Opt-Out of Communications

You can disable push notifications in your device settings or the App's settings.

5.5 Data Portability

You can export all your stickers to your device at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

Stickera is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. By using the App, you consent to this transfer. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

8. Face / Body Data Collection and Use

8.1 What Face or Body Data We Collect

When you use certain AI features such as "3D Cartoon" or similar cartoon or stylized sticker templates, you may upload photos or videos that contain your face or body. These images are provided voluntarily by you for the purpose of creating AI-generated sticker content.

8.2 How Face or Body Data Is Used

  • Primary Purpose: Transform user-uploaded photos into cartoon, stylized, or artistic sticker images
  • Processing Type: Image-to-image AI transformation
  • No Identification: We do NOT attempt to identify who you are, recognize your face, or verify your identity

8.3 AI Processing and Third-Party Services

To generate cartoon or stylized stickers, uploaded images are sent to our third-party AI processing provider FAL.ai, which performs real-time image transformation using its AI models.

  • AI Provider: FAL.ai
  • Purpose: Image-to-image transformation only
  • Model Type: Generative AI (e.g., cartoon or stylized rendering)
  • FAL.ai Privacy Policy: https://fal.ai/privacy

8.4 Data Storage and Retention

  • Temporary Storage: Uploaded photos are temporarily stored in our secure Supabase storage solely for processing purposes (typically no longer than 10 minutes)
  • Automatic Deletion: Original uploaded photos are automatically deleted after AI processing is completed
  • Result Storage: Only the final AI-generated sticker (cartoon or stylized image) is stored in your account
  • Third-Party Retention: FAL.ai processes images in real time and does not retain input images beyond the processing window

8.5 What We Do NOT Do

  • ❌ Do not perform face recognition or facial identification
  • ❌ Do not create biometric identifiers, facial embeddings, or faceprints
  • ❌ Do not store original face photos permanently
  • ❌ Do not use face data for advertising or profiling
  • ❌ Do not sell or share face data with advertisers or data brokers

8.6 User Control and Deletion

You have full control over your content. You can delete any generated stickers at any time from the Gallery. If you request account deletion, all associated sticker data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

9. AI-Generated Content Privacy

9.1 Content Ownership

  • You own the stickers you create
  • We do not claim ownership of your AI-generated content
  • We may use anonymized generation data to improve AI models

9.2 Public vs. Private Content

  • All stickers are stored in cloud storage with unique URLs
  • URLs are not publicly listed or searchable
  • Anyone with the URL can access the sticker (similar to unlisted YouTube videos)
  • Do not share sticker URLs if you want to keep content private

10. Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use cookies. We may use:

  • Local storage for app preferences and cache
  • Session tokens for authentication
  • Anonymous analytics to improve the App

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
